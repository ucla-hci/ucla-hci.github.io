Mission

Our mission is to augment human intelligence through symbiotic computing—embedding AI throughout expert workflows to amplify human abilities, overcome human limitations, and enable human-AI teams to outperform either humans or AI working alone.

UCLA HCI Research is part of Electrical & Computer Engineering Department and Computer Science Department in the Henry Samueli School of Engineering at UCLA.
